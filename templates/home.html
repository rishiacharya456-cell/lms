Company Overview

About Unipick Pvt. Ltd.

Unipick Pvt. Ltd. is a complete IT solutions company based in Kathmandu, Nepal. We provide a full spectrum of technology services — from custom software development and hardware supply to networking infrastructure and cloud management.

Our team of experienced engineers, developers, and consultants work closely with businesses and institutions to understand their unique challenges and deliver scalable, reliable solutions that drive real results.

  • Registered company based in Kathmandu, Nepal

Program Overview

IoT & Robotics for Schools

Nexolabs Academy delivers structured, curriculum-aligned STEM programs directly into partner schools across Nepal.

⚡

IoT Program

Students learn the fundamentals of Internet of Things — sensors, microcontrollers, connectivity, and building real smart devices.

🤖

Robotics Program

Hands-on robotics from basic mechanics to programming — students build, code, and compete with real robots they create themselves.

📚

Curriculum Design

Age-appropriate, structured lessons aligned with national STEM goals, from Grade 5 introduction to Grade 10 advanced projects.

🏆

Competitions & Showcases

Inter-school robotics competitions and project showcases to celebrate and motivate student achievement.

How It Works

Simple. Flexible. Impactful.

Partnering with Nexolabs Academy is designed to be seamless for your school administration.

1

Initial Inquiry

Fill out our partnership form or contact us directly. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your school's needs.

2

Needs Assessment

Our team visits your school, assesses facilities, student grade levels, and proposes the best program fit.

3

Nexolabs handles equipment delivery, lab setup, and instructor deployment. You focus on your students.
